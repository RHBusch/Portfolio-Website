How Snackzz helps you get fresh new snacks

Every year, precious resources are wasted developing new snacks. Companies need an outlet to test their snacks with discerning potential customers. Is this flavor a hit? Is this bag as annoying as the Sunchips bag from the early 2000s? Snackzz is designed to get new products into the hands of potential customers for feedback pre-distribution.
